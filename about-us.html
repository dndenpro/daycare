About ProEves The Best Preschools, Playschools And Daycare Centre Services in India For Kids

Proeves is the leading platform for early childhood care and education (ECCE) and focused on three problems for parents regarding preschool/ daycares

  • Accessibility: discovery efficiency, parent counselling, booking support for preschools & daycares
  • Credibility: standardisation, safety / quality verification, parent references across ProEves Edge centres
  • Flexibility: multiformat phygital support (physical centre, digital and at home support)

We list 7000+ centres across India, and also are building efficiency in the network through our flagship ProEves Edge Centres where we provide Sales, Software & Curriculum partnership to preschool/daycare centres. Centres are powered up on FOUR pillars

Physical set up Support

Content and curriculum

Marketing & Admission

Ketika Kapoor - Co-Founder

I was a consultant and complete #Workaholic- crazy work hours, impossible deadlines & demanding clients were part of the game. Life was fast & furious and I loved it. Just when I wanted to push higher, my little angel came to this world. Her adorable smile and affection transformed me completely. Like every mom, I wanted to provide her with the best care without giving up on my professional aspirations. Not willing to give up I looked for good daycares with flexible/spontaneous services and that’s when I realized how difficult it is to find the right one. Being the#Ambitious #Problemsolver person I am, I thought of creating an accessible, flexible & trustworthy Childcare ecosystem with the help of other women entrepreneurs who are a part of our network. I have over 17 years of experience having worked with Aon Hewitt.

Divya Agarwal - Co-Founder

I had a successful corporate career & was enjoying the festival called “Life”. It got even better with arrival of my beautiful baby girl. Determined to strike a balance, got back to work but found myself stretching at home & work. Being the Doting mother and committed employee I felt I was missing something. Blessed with a sunshine boy, my resolve to make things work doubled. Heading gender diversity at one of the leading companies, I tackled challenges that women faced from close quarters. The idea of simplifying early learning and care for parents clicked instantly and this started the genesis of ProEves with my co founder. I have over 17 years of experience having worked with Aon Hewitt, Unilever South Asia.
